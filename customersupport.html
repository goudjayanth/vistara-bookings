What is Vistara Bookings?

Vistara Bookings is an online platform to list and book venues & farmhouses for weddings, parties, and other special events.

How do I register my venue or farmhouse?

Click on "Service Hub" on the homepage, fill in the form with your venue details, upload images, and submit.

Can I update my venue information after listing?

Yes, you can update or delete your listing at any time through your dashboard.

Is there any fee to register my venue?

Currently, we offer free registrations. Premium features may have a small fee in the future.

How do I book a venue?

Browse the main section, pick a venue, and click "Book Now" to begin the booking process.
